About

Ishica Rana

I work at the intersection of visual art and cinematic storytelling.

While pursuing my degree in Animation and Multimedia, I was determined to become part of film production. As I was good in management. I began working in the event industry in 2022 alongside my college studies — I wanted to be part of the field in any way I could, drawn to the energy of execution teams and the process of turning ideas into real experiences. Working on events strengthened my skills in coordination, management, and understanding how large productions come together behind the scenes.

As my goal was always to work in film, I continued seeking opportunities on sets, eventually working with the costume department and gaining experience as an intern with direction teams. These roles allowed me to observe filmmaking from within the process and understand how different departments collaborate to build a story on screen.

At the same time, I kept nurturing my creative side through design and visual work, ensuring that my artistic perspective continued to grow alongside my practical experience.

With a background spanning event coordination, graphic design, assistant direction, and costume work, my perspective is built from the inside out. I understand production not just as a viewer, but as someone who has lived inside its process.

Currently seeking opportunities in film production and creative direction — bringing a multidisciplinary eye to every story I help tell.
